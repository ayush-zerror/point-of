About

Point Of is a strategic design consultancy based in Bombay, redefining how brands engage with culture and consumers. We work at the intersection of design, strategy, and technology—crafting identities that balance innovation with timeless storytelling. Building for India and beyond.

Our Expertise

We build purposeful, distinctive brands
rooted in strategy, storytelling, and
design.

Scalable websites designed that combine
brand clarity with seamless digital
functionality.

Crafting data-driven marketing strategies
that amplify your brand’s reach, growth
and impact.

From packaging to posters, we design
real-world touchpoints that carry your
brand forward.

Marketing strategies that create impactful
connections, positioning brands to lead
influence within their respective industries.

Media production that captures conveys
brand narratives with precision, ensuring
clarity engagement across platforms.

unlocking growth through design.
