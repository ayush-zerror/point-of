About

Point Of is an independent brand consultancy where ideas design a better future.

Based in Bombay & working globally, we redene how brands engage with culture & consumers creating impact at the intersection of design, strategy, and technology.

Our Expertise

Branding

Every strong brand begins with clarity. We work closely with you to define your brand’s foundation—purpose, values, ambition—and provide thoughtful guidance rooted in insight and experience. Our strategy sessions help uncover what truly sets your brand apart and how to express it meaningfully, internally and out in the world.

Website

We dive deep into your category, culture, and competition. Through a mix of qualitative and quantitative research, we identify opportunities, patterns, and points of tension. This groundwork gives us a clear picture of where your brand stands today and what it needs to grow and stand out tomorrow.

Marketing

From defining your brand’s role in the world to choosing the right name to express it, we help you position your brand with purpose. Our approach to naming combines cultural context, creative exploration, and linguistic rigour to ensure your brand has the clarity and character to connect.

Print

We shape the way your brand speaks and structures itself. Whether it’s streamlining a product suite or finding the right narrative to express your brand personality, we create verbal identities and brand architectures that are easy to understand—and hard to ignore.
